What We Do

Stone Crafter's & Home is a local business that provides kitchen and bathroom design and remodeling.

We are dedicated to meeting the needs of our clients with the professionalism and unparalleled efficiency they expect from the #1 kitchen and bath countertops design facility in the Central Valley. Our staff is dedicated to providing you with an array of choices when it comes to your design needs, your budget, and your personal style. With the best choices available from the leading Granite and Stone manufacturers. Specializing Quartz, Granite and Porcelain Countertops Tile and Solid Surface Showers Flooring.

Our Mission

Established in 2016.

Since we opened our doors and began designing and fabricating countertops and remodels, we have been locally owned and operated in the beautiful city of Modesto, CA. We truly pride ourselves in our superior craftsmanship of countertops and customer service that is second to none. We strive to be the premier source for custom fabrication of quality solid surface products and natural stone.

Our business was founded on the highest standards of excellence; a tradition we extend to each client.
